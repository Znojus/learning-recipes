Very Brown Brownie

Description

This is that super duper ultra chocolaty brownie you've been looking for.

One of the greatest value-for-effort recipes out there.

Ingredients

  • 200g of good quality chocolate, not less than 60% cocoa
  • 200g of butter
  • 200g of sugar
  • 1 tbsp of wheat flour
  • 5 eggs

Steps

  1. Break the chocolate, dice the butter, put these two into a bowl and put it in a microwave until chocolate and butter becomes one mass.
  2. Pour the sugar into the mix and start stirring.
  3. One by one put the eggs into the mix while stirring the mix.
  4. Pour in the wheat flour and stir thoroughly once again.
  5. Pour the mix into a 20 cm unfastening-mold and bake for 20 min.
  6. Check the cake, it should appear uncooked and runny. In this case bake for 2 more min and check again.
  7. You will know that the cake is done when the cake moves just a little after moving the mold.
  8. Take the cake out of the oven and let it rest.
  9. Bon apetit!
