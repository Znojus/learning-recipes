2am Fancy Red Wine Pasta

Description

This one goes for the late night bloomers. If you want something simple, yet fancy, this is the one to go for.

This is a healthy option that only takes about 30 minutes to cook

Ingredients

  • 500 g lean ground beef
  • 2 tbps extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1.5 cups of fresh mushrooms (chopped)
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsps of tomato paste
  • 2 cups of beef stock
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 0.5 cup of red wine (merlot preferred)
  • 0.4 kg penne pasta
  • sea salt and pepper to taste
  • some fresh basil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook the onions, mushrooms, and garlic until the onion is translucent and the mushrooms are softened
  2. Add in the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the tomato paste and combine with the beef and onions. Add the crushed tomatoes, beef stock, and wine. Stir until well combined. Mix in the penne pasta and bring the mixture to a boil.
  4. Stir in the chopped fresh basil, and season to taste with salt and pepper. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Add 1 cup of tomato sauce to the pan and mix until thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes. Serve immediately and enjoy!
